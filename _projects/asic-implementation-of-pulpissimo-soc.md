My bachelor's graduation project, completed in collaboration with Si-Vision, focused on the ASIC implementation of the open-source PULPissimo RISC-V system-on-chip. I used Verilog, TCL, Synopsys EDA tools, and KLayout in an RTL-to-GDSII backend flow covering synthesis, floorplanning, placement, routing, static timing analysis, physical verification, and sign-off.
