This project studied a micromachining-compatible optical MEMS accelerometer based on displacement-sensitive optical readout. I used COMSOL Multiphysics for modelling and characterized the device through mechanical vibration testing, a PLV50 vacuum probe station, a stroboscopic module, reflection digital holographic microscopy, camera-based motion recording, and ImageJ displacement extraction.
