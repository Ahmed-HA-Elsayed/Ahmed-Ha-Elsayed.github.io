A replication and numerical investigation of Stabilizing Remote Entanglement via Waveguide Dissipation. I implement Lindblad master-equation simulations in Python and QuTiP, using NumPy, SciPy, and Matplotlib for parameter sweeps and steady-state and time-domain analysis of concurrence and fidelity under loss, dephasing, drive-strength, and waveguide-phase variations.
